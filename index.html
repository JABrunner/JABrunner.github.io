My name is Jordan

I'm a Programmer and Graphic Designer

In 1992, I was born to two farmers in rural Wisconsin. As young as 5 years old I had a fascination with computers and video games. So much so I've wanted make my own. In sixth grade I was diagnosed with Aspergers, most look at the and think of it as a disability To me, it's my super power, while at a young age it kept me from being organized or kept me from staying focus, I learned to "gamify" thing's I do and in doing so I find solutions to problems some might choose to give up on. I graduated from Lake Area Technical Institute in 2020 with a degree Computer Information Systems In addition to programming I've also been known to dabble in some graphic design and video editting.

Python Space Shooter

In python, I created a space shooter with the help of a tutorial. While this part was easy, when it came to adding to this project, I noticed no audio was present. So I took to adding some sound effects and music. With a bit of effort, I was successful.

QR Code Genorator

Another fun project, I made a QR code generator that will take an input and create that input and make it a QR code. It can be anything, a random string of characters, a hidden message or even a link. For example the QR Code present is functional and if you were to scan the QR with your phone, it will redirect to another website.
