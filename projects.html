QR Code Generator App in C#

A fun side project as I believed making QR codes would be pretty diffcult
that is until I found out that a whole module exists just for making QR Codes.
This QR code generator will convert text or even a link into a QR code
that can be scanned. Text will show up a push message and a link will
also bring up a push message which will take you to said website
